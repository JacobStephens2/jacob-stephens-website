I migrated Rose River Way Studio's website from their original free Wix site to a WordPress site. I expanded and revised the styling of the site per the client's vision and my own ideas. I added requested features including a shop, paid appointment scheduling, and paid event registration. I made a PNG version of the logo using Photoshop to use as the site logo and favicon.

I wrote PHP, CSS, JavaScript, HTML, and English in the site creation process.
